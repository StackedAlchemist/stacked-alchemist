⚗ The Mission

A space where people
are not judged.

Not by anything. Not by anyone. For any reason whatsoever.

The Problem

The entry point is broken.

22
veterans die by suicide every day.

Not because they're weak. Because the entry point to getting help is completely broken.

You're supposed to sit down with a complete stranger and immediately be vulnerable? Most people — especially people built to push through — will never do that. And it's not just veterans. It's everyone who was raised to deal with it and move on. Everyone who carries a filing cabinet full of locked-away things that seep through the cracks when life gets hard enough.

There is nowhere safe for those people to go.

Until now.

Vault · Mission App

Private. Judgment-free.
Yours alone.

Vault is a private journaling and emotional processing app. The core promise: complete privacy, zero judgment, no clinical language.

The AI companion talks like a trusted friend — direct, real, no corporate wellness speak. You journal privately first. You build trust. Then — only if you want to — it can help connect you with someone. On your terms. At your pace.

🔒
Zero Data Storage
All entries are encrypted on your device using AES-256. The encryption key never leaves your device. Not even we can read it.
🤝
AI That Talks Like a Friend
No clinical language. No diagnoses. No "have you tried gratitude journaling." Just real, direct conversation from something that actually listens.
🌉
Soft Therapy Bridge
After consistent use, if heavy patterns emerge, the AI gently asks once if you'd want to talk to someone. If you say no — it never brings it up again.
📋
The Brief
If you ever want to see a professional, Vault generates a session summary so your first appointment starts somewhere real — not at square one.
🎖️
Veteran Mode
Verified via ID.me. Free forever, no questions asked. The AI switches to a direct, no-nonsense voice — full respect for the "handle your own shit" mentality.
🤜
Battle Buddy
Verified veterans can opt into peer matching. The app connects two vets based on service era and what they're processing. Anonymous. In-app. Real.
🎖️ Veteran Early Access

You served. This is the least we can do. Sign up for early access — free forever, no strings attached.

Your information is never sold or shared. This signup is only used to notify you when Vault launches.

The Compound

But Vault is just the beginning.

The long-term vision is physical communities in all 50 states. Real homes for displaced veterans and homeless individuals. Not shelters. Not programs. Homes — with dignity built into the architecture.

🏠
Residential BuildingsPrivate rooms and shared spaces for residents.
🛡️
Veteran-Staffed SecurityEmployment and community in one. Veterans protecting veterans.
📚
On-Site SchoolsGED programs, vocational training, life skills — inside the compound.
🛒
Community StoreGrocery and convenience — operated by and for residents.
🧠
Mental Health CenterTherapists, psychologists, counselors on site. No commute. No barrier.

The Path

This is how we get there.

1
Build revenue apps that people actually need. Real products solving real problems. Every subscription funds the mission.
2
File the 501(c)(3) nonprofit. Under $500 to do it ourselves. No excuses. The paperwork is already mapped.
3
Launch Vault — free for everyone. The mental health app ships under the nonprofit umbrella the moment the filing is approved.
4
Apply for VA grants, federal health tech grants, foundation funding. The VA Grant and Per Diem Program. SAMHSA. Bob Woodruff Foundation. The money exists.
5
Break ground on the first compound. One facility. One state. Proof of concept. Document everything.
6
Do it again in every state. The blueprint scales. The mission doesn't stop until there are 50.

This was never a pipe dream.

Pipe dreams don't have blueprints.
