Tugging and pulling I only fall deeper

Deeper and deeper I fall, trying to reach salvation

Trying I only seem to fail

To fail my dream start to fade

Fading dreams are resulting in my devastation

Devastated I scream for salvation

Salvation just out of my grasp, I fall deeper

The deeper I go chains start to wrap around me

Chains are gripping then ripping and twisting about my skin

The flesh tears from my body as the chains twist tighter

I try and I beg but yet I keep falling

Falling I start to give

Devastation by cremation my end is near

Salvation is near

Reaching out it once again slips past

Struggling and trying my life is coming to a close

The struggling and crying is now over

Looking up salvation is yet again near

Bowing my head I close my eyes and ask

I ask for forgiveness and help

Now as I lay my soul for the devil to take salvation draws closer

Just in reach, the devil reaches for my soul

Salvation comes and lifts me up

Now out of reach, I smile

Smiling and happiness come back

With happiness come my dreams

I am now Saved.
