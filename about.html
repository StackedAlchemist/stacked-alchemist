Origin Story

Who I Am

I'm Billy Williams — born October 6th, 1983 in Orange County, California, raised in Arizona since I was 10. I'm a developer, security professional, lifelong builder, and the author of The Winglord Saga.

I came to code the same way I came to building PCs — through pure curiosity and a stubborn need to understand how things actually work. I built my first rig in 2005 and never looked back. Hardware taught me that every great machine is made of small, well-chosen parts working in harmony. That same thinking drives how I write code.

My background is in security — years of staying sharp, reading environments, and keeping calm when things go sideways. Turns out that translates perfectly to debugging at 2am. I also studied game development at Full Sail University before life took a few sharp turns, and I've been building toward software development seriously ever since.

I'm a husband and father of three. Family is the reason I build. Every line of code is an investment in something better.

Gaming Roots

Gaming isn't just a hobby — it's part of who I am. I was a StarCraft obsessive, played Diablo since day one, and when World of Warcraft launched it consumed years of my life. I still hop on today, just with a bit more restraint.

There's something permanently nostalgic about Mario Bros, Zelda, and Final Fantasy. FFVII changed how I think about storytelling. IX, X, and XIV all hit differently. The FFVII Remake blew me away. I'm a Nintendo and PlayStation person at heart — but I game on PC these days so I don't monopolize the TV.

Rocket League still gets my competitive itch. Always hunting the next game that grabs me the way those did.
